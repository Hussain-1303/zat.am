About the game

श्रीमद्-भगवद्गीता (Shriimad-BhagavadGiitaa) has 46 names for Krishna and 26 names for Arjuna (as listed on http://bhagavadgita.org.in/names). Just knowing them and identifying them while reciting shloka helps a lot in understanding.

This “word search” puzzle contains names of Krishna and Arjuna that are used in श्रीमद्-भगवद्गीता.

How to use the game

Notice you can have a puzzle with 5x5, 7x7, 10x10 matrix by clicking below. Advanced section talks more about different ways to create different combinations.

You have two options to generate a puzzle. Use above button to chose your selection:

        Case-1: Just Krishna’s name

        Case-2: Just Arjuna’s name

Way: on computer/phone

Once you spot a name of Krishna/Arjun, if you click on the first letter of the name, you shall see the whole word (name) getting highlighted on screen.

Example: If you just click on कु below, it shall highlight the whole word (Kurunandan - a name of Arjun). If you select any other letter in the middle, it will not highlight the word.

TIP: For those who are not sure about Devanagari lipi, once you hover a mouse on a letter, it shall display how to pronounce it in Roman transliteration (English) format.

Way: on paper

From a computer/phone, you can just print the puzzle.

This is a very good idea when you want to stay away from the screen.

Advanced

Puzzle in different formats

When you click on the URL, you will see “size” in it. Example:

https://zat.am/007-ask/index.html?size=7&m=a 

Above is giving you a puzzle in 7x7 format.

You can change the size to any other number and it will give you a puzzle in that format.

Limitation: No reproducibility

Every time you refresh and get a new puzzle, it is randomly creating a puzzle. At this point, you can not reproduce the same puzzle easily. Plan is to attach number with puzzle (like puzzle-id) and with that user shall be able to reproduce the same easily in future

Limitation: Solution not available

It is not showing all names in the puzzle.

Plan is to give the option to list names used in the puzzle. That way users can find words in the puzzle.
